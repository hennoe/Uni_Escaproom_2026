CYBER ATTACK

5:00 PM – Meadow behind the HSZ

The bass from the band Tapy vibrates in your chest, while the smell of charcoal and freedom drifts across the campus. But for you, this Dies Academicus is anything but relaxing. You have a mission.

You spot the security guard at the edge of the barrier. He looks visibly stressed, rubbing his temples.

"Any progress?" you ask, raising your voice to be heard over the music. "Do we have updates on the five suspects?"

He shakes his head and pulls a crumpled report out of his pocket. "It's absolute chaos here. We know it was five people who hacked into the systems. Each sat at a station with a different security level (Red, Green, Yellow, Blue, White), used a different device (Notebook, Tablet, PC, Server Terminal, Smartphone), and a different connection (VPN, Proxy, WLAN, LAN, Cellular)."

He points to a list of secured evidence: "We found five distinct clues at the scene: a fired confetti cannon, a spilled Mate, an empty pizza box, glitter stickers everywhere, and a magazine with funny cat pictures."

"There's at least one piece of good news," he adds, pointing to a surveillance video showing an unidentifiable person in a hooded sweatshirt laughing while looking at the cat magazine. "The person with the cat pictures is definitely innocent. We had them on camera the entire time: they were so captivated by the fluffy kittens that they didn't look at the monitor for a single second – they literally didn't have a hand free for the hack. We just need to figure out who that was."

Before you can ask any further questions, you are pulled away by a group of students. Arriving in room FOE 102, you catch crucial statements amidst the dancing crowd:

Solve the cyber riddle and find the connections.

🟢🟢 Very Very Easy

Someone sorted the statements and interviewed a lot of witnesses. Very quick to solve. In case you don't feel like a heavy challenge.

🟢 Easy

Someone sorted the statements and gathered a few additional clues. Ideal for getting started in the world of riddles.

🟡 Medium

Someone sorted the statements for you to make it easier to solve. Use logic and the clues.

🔴 Hard

All statements are mixed up. No table provided. Caution!! Only recommended if you are experienced with logic puzzles and have enough time left!
